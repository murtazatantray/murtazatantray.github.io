Welcome to my academic homepage. I am a full-time Research Scholar at the School of Advanced Science (SAS) at VIT, Vellore, working under the supervision of Dr. Padigepati Naveen.

My research lies at the intersection of **Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)** and scientific machine learning. I specifically focus on the boundary layer flow of non-Newtonian. To solve these complex fluid mechanics problems, I develop and implement **Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)**, bridging the gap between traditional numerical methods and modern deep learning frameworks.

### Research Interests
* Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
* Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)

### Technical Skills
* **Scientific Computing:** Python, MATLAB, PyTorch, DeepXDE
* **Infrastructure & Tools:** High-Performance Computing (Slurm), LaTeX, Matplotlib
